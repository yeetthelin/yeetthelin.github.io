About Me!

Hi! I'm Yilin Liao and I'm a rising senior at St. Theresa of Lisieux Catholic High School. I am very interested in anything relating to computer science, engineering, applied math, physics, and biomechanics! Outside of academia, I am involved in a variety of extracurriculars in school such as Intramural Council (President), Presidents' Council (1 of 9 students representing the student body), VEX Robotics (3 years) and varsity athletics (Track & Field and Cross Country). I am also a national level short track speed skater who represented Team Ontario at the Canada Winter Games. I'm so excited that you're here! Scroll down to learn more :)

2023 Canada Winter Games in Charlottetown, PEI

In February 2023, I represented Ontario at the Canada Winter Games. It was two weeks full of ups and downs but I ultimately learned so much from it. I didn't allow it to affect me and maintained a strong and determined mindset. With my teammates, I won the gold medal in the 3000m Women’s Relay, marking the first time Ontario ever beat Québec in the event.
